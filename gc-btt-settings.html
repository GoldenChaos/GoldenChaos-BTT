Menu Bar

Dynamic Menu Bar

Allows you to switch between large, medium, and small menu bar sizes on the fly.

Primary Menu Bar Widgets

These menu bar widgets stay docked to the right even when holding modifier keys.

When set to "Dynamic", these widgets will disappear on medium and small menu bar sizes.

When set to "Dynamic", the battery and weather widgets will be hidden on small menu bar sizes.

Date and Time

When set to "Dynamic", the date will automatically switch between
large, medium, and small formats when you resize the menu bar.

Volume and Brightness

Keys for volume and brightness will always appear in the Control Strip,
even if you disable them in the menu bar.

Secondary Menu Bar Widgets

These widgets disappear when holding down modifier keys.

Only appears when running apps that use the microphone.

Automatically looks for and connects to Bluetooth devices containing the words "AirPods", "Beats", "Bose", etc. Requires the Bluetooth menu to be enabled in the macOS menu bar.

Supports Safari, Safari Technology Preview, Google Chrome, and Chrome Canary.

Requires the Floating Window Snapping Grid to be enabled in the "General" tab.

Dock Badges

Dock Badges allow you to see notification badges from apps in your dock, including from Handoff devices.
